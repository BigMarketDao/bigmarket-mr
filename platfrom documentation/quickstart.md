# Quickstart

From zero to your first prediction in four steps.

---

## Before you start

You need two things before you can trade on BigMarket:

1. A wallet — Xverse, Leather, or Asigna. If you haven't set one up yet, start with the [wallet setup guide in BigMarket 101 →]()
2. Some STX — the token used across most markets. You can buy STX on Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance and send it to your wallet.

Once you have both, the rest takes about two minutes.

---

## Step 1 — Connect your wallet

Go to [bigmarket.ai](https://bigmarket.ai) and click "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner. Your wallet app will open and ask you to approve the connection. Click approve.

You'll see your wallet address and token balance appear in the top right. That's it — you're connected.

Every transaction you make will still require a separate approval in your wallet. Connecting doesn't give BigMarket permission to move your funds freely.

---

## Step 2 — Find a market

Browse the market listing on the homepage or use the search bar to find something you have a view on. Markets are organised by category — sports, crypto, politics, and more.

Click on any market to see the details: the question, the possible outcomes, the current prices, how much is staked, the deadline, and the token the market uses.

A few things to check before you commit:

- **The token** — make sure you hold the token the market runs on. Most markets use STX. If it uses sBTC or USDA, you'll need to acquire those first.
- **The mechanism** — AMM or Knockout. AMM lets you exit early. Knockout locks you in until resolution. This is always shown on the market page.
- **The creator fee** — some markets charge a creator fee of up to 10% on top of the 1% platform fee. It's always displayed before you trade. Factor it into your thinking.
- **The deadline** — when trading closes. After this point, positions are locked.

---

## Step 3 — Place your prediction

Click the outcome you want to back. A trade panel will open on the right side of the screen.

Enter the amount you want to stake. The panel will show you:

- How many shares you'll receive
- The effective price per share at this amount
- The estimated slippage — how much the price will move because of your trade
- The total fees you'll pay

Set your slippage tolerance. This is a safeguard — if the price moves more than your tolerance between when you click and when the transaction confirms, it cancels automatically and nothing is charged. For most trades on active markets, the default is fine. For large trades on thin markets, tighten it.

When you're happy with the numbers, click "Stake" and approve the transaction in your wallet. Wait a few seconds for block confirmation. Your position appears in your dashboard.

---

## Step 4 — Track and manage your position

Go to "My Markets" in your dashboard to see all your open positions. For each one you can see:

- Which outcome you backed and at what price
- The current price of that outcome
- Your current share count and what it would be worth if you sold now
- The market deadline and current state

**If you're in an AMM market and want to exit early:** click "Sell" on your position, enter the number of shares to sell, and confirm. You receive the current value of your shares back in your wallet immediately.

**If you're in a Knockout market:** there's no early exit. You hold until the market resolves.

**When the market resolves:** your position will show "Claim available" if you backed the winning outcome. Click "Claim", approve the transaction, and your winnings arrive in your wallet. There's no deadline to claim — they'll be there whenever you're ready.

---

## What can go wrong?

**My transaction failed.**
Usually caused by slippage — the price moved beyond your tolerance before the transaction confirmed. Nothing was charged. Try again with a slightly wider slippage tolerance, or wait for the market to settle.

**I connected the wrong wallet.**
Disconnect from the top right menu and reconnect with the correct one. Your positions are tied to your wallet address, not your browser session.

**I can't see my position after staking.**
Wait for block confirmation — it usually takes under a minute. If it's been longer, check the transaction in your wallet's history. If it shows confirmed, refresh the market page.

**The market I want uses a token I don't have.**
You'll need to acquire that token before you can participate. STX is available on most major exchanges. sBTC and USDA can be acquired through exchanges that support Stacks ecosystem tokens.
