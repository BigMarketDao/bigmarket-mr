# Fees and Payouts

What you pay, what you earn, and how the numbers work.

---

## What fees does BigMarket charge?

**Is there a fee on every trade?**

Yes. Every trade — buy or sell — has a 1% protocol fee. That's it from the platform side. No hidden charges, no fees that appear at claim time that weren't shown at entry.

The fee is taken from the trade amount at the moment you transact. It's shown in the trade panel before you confirm, so you always know exactly what you're paying.

**What about creator fees?**

Some markets have an additional fee set by the person who created them — between 0% and 10%. Not all markets have one. When a creator fee applies, it's shown clearly on the market page before you enter.

Creator fees and the protocol fee are additive. If a market has a 5% creator fee, your total fee on that market is 6% — 5% to the creator, 1% to the platform. The trade panel always shows the combined total, not the parts separately.

**So what's the maximum I could pay in fees?**

11% — 10% creator fee plus 1% protocol fee. That would be an unusually high creator fee. Most markets will be significantly lower. Check the market page before entering.

**Where does the 1% protocol fee go?**

It splits two ways:

- 30% goes to the liquidity providers in that market — the people who deposited tokens to make trading possible. They earn a small cut of every trade in exchange for taking on the risk of providing liquidity.
- 70% goes to the platform treasury — controlled collectively by the community, used to fund platform development and operations.

Nothing goes to a private company. The treasury is governed by community vote.

## How does my payout work?

**How is a winning payout calculated?**

When a market resolves, the entire pool — everything staked across all outcomes — gets split among the winners. Your share is proportional to how many winning shares you hold relative to all winning shares.

A simple example: a market has 1,000 STX staked in total. 400 STX ended up on the winning outcome. You hold 40 STX worth of shares in that outcome — that's 10% of the winning side. You receive 10% of the total 1,000 STX pool, minus fees.

The earlier you bought into the winning outcome and the fewer people who agreed with you, the larger your share of the pool. That's the reward for being right when others weren't.

**Does fee timing affect my payout?**

The 1% protocol fee is charged when you trade — on the way in and on the way out. The creator fee, if any, is also applied at trade time. By the time a market resolves, fees have already been collected. Your claim is calculated from what's left in the pool after fees.

**What if I sell before resolution?**

You receive whatever your shares are currently worth at the moment of the sale — not a share of the final pool. The same 1% protocol fee and any creator fee apply to the sell transaction. Your payout is determined by the current price of your outcome, not the final resolution.

## What else can I earn on BigMarket?

**Do I earn anything just by participating?**

Yes — reputation. Every meaningful action earns BIG-R reputation points, which convert to BIG tokens at the end of each monthly epoch.

The actions that earn the most are the ones that demonstrate genuine contribution:

| Action | Reputation points |
|--------|-------------------|
| Claiming a winning prediction | 10 |
| Creating a market | 8 |
| Voting in a dispute | 7 |
| Providing liquidity | Scaled to amount |
| Making a prediction | 6 |
| Voting on governance proposals | 3 |

Winning earns the most reputation — because it proves you were right. That's the signal the platform values highest.

**How much BIG can I earn per month?**

The platform distributes a fixed pool of 10,000 BIG every epoch — roughly monthly. Your share depends on your weighted reputation score relative to everyone else who participated that epoch.

There's no guaranteed amount per action. The more active the platform, the more people competing for the same pool. But the pool itself doesn't shrink — it's 10,000 BIG regardless of how many people are participating.

**Is there any cost to earning reputation?**

No. Reputation is a byproduct of doing things you'd do anyway — trading, providing liquidity, voting. There's no separate action required to earn it. It accumulates automatically and converts to BIG automatically at the end of each epoch.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Rate | Applies to | Who receives it |
|-----|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Protocol fee | 1% | Every buy and sell | 30% to LPs in that market, 70% to platform treasury |
| Creator fee | 0–10% | Every buy and sell in that market | Market creator |
| Network gas fee | Small, variable | Every transaction | Stacks network (not BigMarket) |

All fees are shown before you confirm any transaction. Nothing is charged without your explicit approval.
