# Overview

Predict what happens next — and earn when you're right.

---

## The short version

BigMarket is a prediction market platform. You pick an outcome, put money behind it, and if you're right, you earn a share of the pool. Every market runs on rules written in code — no company can change them, no one can alter a result after the fact, and your funds are always in your control.

## How it works

Think of a prediction market as a poll where people back their opinions with money.

A question gets posted — "Will the Champions League final go to penalties?" or "Will BTC close above $100k in June?" — and two or more outcomes open up for people to take positions on. As more people back one outcome over another, that outcome gets more expensive. The crowd's confidence shows directly in the price.

When the event resolves, everyone who backed the correct outcome splits the pool. The more accurate your prediction, and the earlier you made it, the more you earn.

That's the core idea. Everything else on BigMarket is built around making that experience fairer, more trustworthy, and more rewarding than anywhere else.

## Why BigMarket is different

Most platforms that let you trade on outcomes are run by a company. That company sets the rules, takes the fees, decides which markets exist, and can change any of it at any time. You're trusting them to be fair.

BigMarket works differently. The rules are written in code that anyone can read. Fees are fixed and visible before you trade. No single person or company controls the outcome of any market — and if a result is ever disputed, any participant can formally challenge it and the community votes to resolve it.

The platform is governed collectively by the people who use it. The more you participate — trading, providing liquidity, voting on outcomes — the more influence you earn over how the platform evolves. Everyone who contributes earns a stake in the system over time. Not by buying in, but by showing up.

This isn't a new idea dressed up in technical language. It's a fundamentally different way of running an organisation — one where the users are the owners, and the rules can't be quietly changed overnight.

## What makes a result final

Every market on BigMarket resolves through a process that no individual can override.

For most markets, a group of independent agents signals the outcome. Once enough of them agree, the result is recorded. There's then a window — roughly one day — where any participant who disagrees can formally challenge it. If someone does, the community votes. The vote runs for about two and a half days. Whatever the community decides becomes the final, permanent result.

For markets tied to real-world data — like a cryptocurrency price — the result is pulled automatically from a public price feed. No human is involved at all.

Once a result is recorded, it cannot be changed. That's not a policy. It's how the code works.

## Your money, always in your control

When you trade on BigMarket, your funds go directly into the market — not into a BigMarket account. There is no BigMarket account. The platform never holds your money. You can exit most markets at any time before they close, and you can claim your winnings the moment a market resolves.

The fees are simple. Every trade has a 1% protocol fee — a small portion goes to the people who keep the market liquid, the rest funds the platform. If a market was created by an individual creator, they may charge an additional fee of up to 10%, which is always shown before you trade.

Nothing is hidden. Nothing changes after you've committed.

## Who builds this

BigMarket is built and governed by its community. Every major decision — fee rates, which markets are allowed, how disputes are resolved — is made through community votes. The people who participate most earn the most say in those decisions, through a reputation system that can't be bought or transferred. You earn it by contributing. Full stop.
